Inaugural Eastern Region Nursing Students Dinner & Meet-and-Greet

Home News

Date: March 2026

Location: Eastern Uganda

Christ’s Heart Medical Services Limited (CHMSL) successfully hosted its inaugural Eastern Region Nursing Students Dinner & Meet-and-Greet, marking a significant milestone in its commitment to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals.

The event brought together nursing students from various institutions across the Eastern Region, providing a unique opportunity for mentorship, peer interaction, and career guidance.

Speakers emphasized professionalism, ethical practice, community service, and faith-guided compassion in healthcare delivery.

Key Highlights

  • Mentorship sessions with healthcare professionals
  • Networking among nursing students
  • Discussions on career development
  • CHMSL’s vision for student engagement

CHMSL looks forward to organizing similar engagements aimed at empowering healthcare students and strengthening community health systems.
